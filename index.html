About Us

Our mission: To make every client feel confident, refreshed, and cared for through expert grooming and personalized hair services. Since 2015, we grew into a respected grooming destination in Angeles City, Pampanga, particularly known for its convenient location across Holy Angel University. Our salon is owned and operated by Charlie Domingo, a professional hairstylist with certifications and years of experience in the field. Our shop runs as a traditional and modern business, with all services performed in person. We do not sell through e-commerce platforms, but focus instead on building client relationships, maintaining professional standards, and creating a warm, family-like atmosphere inside our salon. Customer concerns or inquiries are mainly handled via our Facebook page, where people can view sample work, promotions, and send messages directly. This personal touch in operations paired with skillful services is what has made Charlie's Salon a local favorite over the past years.

Working Hours

Charlie's Salon is open every day from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM, depending on the number of clients. We welcome walk-ins and also accept appointments through our Facebook page. During busy hours—like weekends and evenings—customers are encouraged to message ahead to avoid waiting. The staff makes sure each client is attended to with care, whether they come in for a quick trim or a full hair makeover. Every visit is handled professionally, making sure customers feel relaxed and satisfied with their service.

Services & Products Offered

We provides a full suite of hair care services for both men and women, blending modern hairstyling trends with classic techniques. Here's what we offer:

Hair Services:

  • Haircuts - From clean fades and layered cuts to textured trims and undercuts, customized for each client.
  • Hair Coloring - Including full-color transformations, highlights, styles, root touch-ups, and trendy tones.
  • Hair Rebonding & Treatments - Smoothing, strengthening, and revitalizing treatments like keratin services and deep-conditioning masks.

Hair Products for Sale:

  • Hair Color Creams
  • Essential Oils & Serums
  • Moisturizing Hair Masks
  • Styling Sprays & Heat Protectants

All products sold are salon-grade, tested, and recommended by the staff to ensure they meet clients' hair care needs even after leaving the salon.
